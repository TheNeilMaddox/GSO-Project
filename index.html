    Solar External Timelines

The goal of this project was to identify easy to operate in markets based on 3rd party barriers. Solar installations tend to have two major third party disruptors. The first is the Utility company, which sets constraints surrounding the interconnection process. The second is the permitting authority which handles permitting & inspection of the solar array.
